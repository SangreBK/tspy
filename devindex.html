How To play

  1. Download and install the Sweet Tea launcher.
  2. The Sweet Tea launcher should be available in your Start Menu.
  3. If Thunderspy (/coxg/) server is not listed,
    1. Go to the launcher's options menu
    2. Add the link to this manifest: https://www.thunderspygaming.net/styles/freedom/manifest.xml
  4. If Sweet Tea doesn't work, try Sunrise Launcher.

About Us

Thunderspy is a City of Heroes private server that is constantly updated with new content and features. Among many new features are original and re-balanced powersets, asymmetric and original costume parts, extra auras, and more.

Despite the rapid improvements, the Thunderspy server maintains a small, friendly community where everyone knows everyone.

Server expenses are $430 (USD) monthly. Help keep us online through Patreon or PayPal.

Code of Conduct

  1. If a player is bothering you, block that player and report to a GM or mod.
  2. Do not try to circumvent another player's attempt to block you.
  3. The in-game word filter is enabled by default. Leave it on if you do not wish to see profanity.
  4. Do not try to circumvent the in-game word filter by purposefully misspelling words or through any other means.
  5. Do not spam or be disruptive. You may be temporarily muted for it.

If you would like to speak to an admin, you can post in the forums.

You can also message a GM or mod in our Discord for a faster and more discrete response.
